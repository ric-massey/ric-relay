ORRIN

Orrin is my main project — over a year in the making. It's a cognitive runtime: a program that runs continuously with its own persistent memory, goals, and attention. The unusual part: the AI language model is the smallest piece of it, a tool Orrin picks up and puts down — not the brain in charge. Orrin can run with no AI model at all.

The point is to study what happens when a system like this runs for a long time: what it pays attention to, how its goals shift, what it chooses to remember and forget. The interesting results come from watching what it actually does, not what it was designed to do.
