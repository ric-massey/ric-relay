Hard boundary

This package does not claim that independence has already happened. A second pass by the same author is not independent replication. Reviewer names remain blank, licensed databases remain unrun, and the provisional result does not become validated merely because the workflow is polished.

Why this wording matters

OSF defines preregistration as a time-stamped, read-only plan posted before the work it governs. The existing author pilot cannot be made preregistered after the fact. The new registration can prospectively govern the independent replication while openly preserving the pilot as prior work.

02 · Protocol lock

The rules are fixed before new reviewers see the answer.

Locked

Evidence question

Screened adults with MDD, one 25 mg oral synthetic-psilocybin administration, monitored clinical setting, controlled comparison, and prespecified 24-hour and seven-day harms.

Locked

Strata

Direct MDD evidence remains separate from TRD, lower/weight-adjusted dose, repeated-dose, fixed-order, and incompatible-comparator evidence.

Locked

Rare-event math

Arm counts first; exact intervals; no continuity correction that creates information; no unstable relative-effect pooling when events are absent.

Locked

Interpretation

No recreational-use generalization, no “risk is zero,” no safety equivalence, and no conversion into a universal 0–100 drug score.

04 · Independent workflow

Independence is implemented as access control, not a promise.

Reviewer 1

First pass

Screens, extracts, and judges RoB 2 without seeing Reviewer 2 or the author-pilot decisions.

Reviewer 2

Independent first pass

Uses the same forms with concealed decisions. Uncertainty moves records forward rather than forcing exclusion.

Adjudicator

Conflict resolution

Resolves only after both passes lock, cites the rule or source used, and preserves the disagreement.

Information specialist

Search peer review

Checks conceptual translation, headings, text words, syntax, filters, and database translations before execution.

06 · Registration gate

The files are ready; the accountable research team is not.

PROSPERO

PROSPERO is an international prospective registry for health-related systematic reviews. A team member must create the record, take responsibility for the review, and keep it updated.

Official registry

OSF

An OSF registration can archive a time-stamped, read-only version of the protocol, forms, code, and manifest. It should explicitly say that the registration governs the independent replication, not the completed author pilot.

Official registration guidance

Release condition: do not change the public label from “single-author pilot” until named independent reviewers complete the workflow and the adjudicated data/code release is posted.
