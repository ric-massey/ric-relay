THE SHAPE OF HARM - v0.9

PUBLIC ENTRY POINT:  start.html
CURRENT RESEARCH GATE: hardening.html

1. start.html is the plain-language front door: what the numbers mean, what they don't,
   and where to go next. It is generated from scores.csv by build_start_page.py.
2. index.html is the interactive ranking. Set your own weights; the order changes.
3. The message is: every substance here carries real harm, and they differ enormously
   in kind and degree. It is NOT "all drugs are equally dangerous" - the data refutes that.
4. Equal weighting is used only to order rows on start.html, and is labelled as one
   choice among many. The model itself refuses to privilege any weighting.
5. A blank score is never a zero. Withheld values are shown as withheld.
6. The only released estimate (0/67, upper limit 53.6 per 1,000) is a SECONDARY
   attribution-based outcome. The primary outcome, all-cause SAE, is not estimable.
7. Check document-status-register.csv before using any file.
8. Run: python3 validate_site.py && python3 validate_launch.py && python3 validate_hardening.py
9. Not independently reviewed. Not prospectively registered. No completed search.
